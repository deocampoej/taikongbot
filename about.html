Quality Gypsum Boards for a lower Price!

Being the manufacturer, taikongbot® has been making gypsum boards for other brands to stick their logos on so that they can sell it for three times the price. This came to a stop when we decided to set up our own brand and sell directly to customers that would actually use the products.

We want our customers to get the gypsum boards with a lower price and yet without any compromise on the quality. Working with people in the Philippines, taikongbot® is able to answer to customers immediately and arrange the shipment within 24 hours when an order is made.

No matter who you are, we would like to talk to you in person and try our very best to make your life easier.
